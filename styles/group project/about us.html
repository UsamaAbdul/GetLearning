Our Story

Founders story

About 1 year ago, Mahmud, Issak, Osama and Mazen started designing a website called GetLearning. This site aims to collect donations and distribute them to schools facing the problems of lack of educational materials. Their focus was on students and teachers, but on the side, they created an email marketing service to attract both small and large businesses. MailLearning was designed as an alternative to the bulky and expensive email program in the early 2000s. This service has allowed the site to grow and reach the groups in need of support through small and large business owners.
